Frequently Asked Questions

What is the starting price?
The starting price for our luxury residences begins at ₹2.5 Crore for a 2BHK configuration. Prices vary based on floor, view, and specific unit features.
What amenities are included?
All residents have access to our world-class amenities including infinity pool, glass-walled gym, rooftop sky lounge, premium clubhouse, smart home automation, 24/7 concierge services, spa & wellness center, and private parking pods.
Is there a payment plan?
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans tailored to suit your financial requirements. Our relationship managers can provide detailed information about various payment options during your site visit.
Are site visits available?
Absolutely. We encourage prospective residents to experience our property firsthand. Private site tours can be scheduled by contacting our sales team or through the "Book a Visit" option on our website.
When is the possession date?
The possession for our current phase is scheduled for Q4 2025. We maintain strict timelines and provide regular construction updates to our customers.
